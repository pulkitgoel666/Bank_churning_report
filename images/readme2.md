Images Directory README
Overview

This directory contains visualizations generated during the data analysis project on account closures. The images provide graphical representations of key findings, helping to summarize insights and communicate trends effectively.
Contents

    bar_chart_account_closures.png: A bar chart showing the distribution of account closures by gender, illustrating that 54.5% of closures are from male account holders.
    doughnut_chart_credit_scores.png: A doughnut chart displaying the account closures based on credit scores, highlighting that 34% of closures are from individuals with fair credit scores, followed by 26% with low credit scores.
    pie_chart_age_groups.png: A pie chart representing account closures by age group, indicating that most closures occur in the 30-60 age range, making up 78% of the total.
    geographic_distribution_chart.png: A geographic chart illustrating the account closure rates by location, revealing that nearly 50% of closures are from customers in France.

Usage

These images can be used to:

    Enhance presentations by providing visual context for the data analysis findings.
    Support reports or summaries with graphical evidence of trends and patterns.
    Serve as references for further discussions on the factors influencing account closures.

How to View

You can view the images directly in this directory or download them for use in your presentations or reports.
